## LiteAPI Setup - Pros & Cons

### Pros
- You control your markup (not fixed commission)
- Access to millions of hotels globally
- No upfront inventory costs
- Weekly payouts after guest checkout

### Cons
- Competing with Booking.com's 25-40% affiliate commission

- Zero traffic = zero revenue

## What You Need to Get Right

### 1. Traffic First, Platform Second

Your beautiful site means nothing without visitors. Options:

- **Content/SEO** (slow, 6-12 months, but free)
- **Paid ads** (expensive: $24+/booking CAC)
- **Existing audience** (social following, email list)
- **Partnerships** (travel agents, influencers)

### 2. Pick a Niche

Generic "book any hotel" = losing to Booking.com. Instead:

- Safari lodges in Southern Africa
- Remote work hotels (monthly stays)
- Halal-friendly hotels
- Digital nomad destinations
- Wedding/honeymoon venues

### 3. Value Beyond Booking

Why should someone use you over Booking.com?

- Curated recommendations (AI chat is a start)
- Local expertise content
- Package deals (flights + hotels + experiences)
- Loyalty/cashback program

### 4. Unit Economics Must Work

```
Typical hotel booking: $150/night x 2 nights = $300
Your markup: 10% = $30 revenue
After payment processing (3%): ~$21 net

To make $5,000/month profit:
Need ~240 bookings/month
At 2% conversion: Need 12,000 visitors/month
```

## Recommended Plan

### Phase 1: Validate (2-4 weeks)

1. Pick ONE niche (suggest: South African safari lodges or remote work hotels)
2. Create 10 pieces of SEO content for that niche
3. Test with $200-500 paid ads to gauge conversion
4. **Success metric**: 5+ bookings with <$30 CAC

### Phase 2: Double Down or Pivot (Month 2-3)

- If working: Scale content + ads
- If not: Try different niche or B2B model (sell to travel agents)

### Phase 3: Differentiate (Month 3-6)

- Add unique features (WhatsApp booking, local experiences)
- Build email list for repeat bookings
- Partner with influencers/bloggers

---

## Verdict

### Worth Pursuing IF:
- You have an existing audience or traffic source
- You focus on a specific niche
- You treat it as a long-term content play (6-12 months)
- You can handle $0 revenue for first 3-6 months

### Don't Bother IF:
- You expect quick returns
- You're competing generically with OTAs
- You don't have marketing budget or content skills

**Realistic expectation**: This is a side project that *could* become a business, not a startup that will take off quickly.
